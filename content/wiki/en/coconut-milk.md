## Why It Matters for Longevity

Recommended unsweetened plant milk for the Longevity Diet. Fortified coconut milk is a top source of calcium (451 mg per cup, 45% DV) and vitamin D (101 IU per cup, 25% DV). Recommended serving: 1 glass (240 mL); choose unsweetened; calcium- and vitamin-D-fortified versions preferred. Plant-based calcium and vitamin D source when fortified; supports bone mineral density and vitamin D status without the saturated fat and IGF-1-raising effects of cow's milk dairy..  Fortified plant milks (including coconut milk) provide bioavailable calcium and vitamin D comparable to cow's milk, supporting bone health outcomes relevant to fall prevention and fracture risk reduction in aging populations. (Appleton et al., Nutrients (2018) — PMID 30513795)  Plant-based milk alternatives fortified with calcium and vitamin D represent viable substitutes for dairy in populations seeking to reduce IGF-1 and saturated fat intake while maintaining adequate bone-protective micronutrient status. (Wallace et al., Nutrients (2020) — PMID 32272666)

## Synergies

- **Turmeric** (synergy): Coconut milk fat dramatically increases the bioavailability of curcumin from turmeric; the classic golden milk combination is nutritionally well-founded.  - **Oats** (complement): Coconut milk provides calcium and vitamin D to complement oat beta-glucan's cholesterol-lowering action, creating a breakfast combining cardiovascular and bone-health benefits.

## Flavor Profile

Taste: mildly sweet, creamy, subtly coconutty. Aroma: tropical, lightly nutty, faintly sweet. Texture: creamy, smooth, slightly viscous. Category: plant-based milk / liquid ingredient.
